Zihao Wang is a Ph.D. student from [CSE department](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/), [HKUST](https://hkust.edu.hk/) since Sept. 2020. He is advised by [Prof. Yangqiu Song](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~yqsong/). The central topic of his research is reasoning especially with the symbolic knowledge. He is also interested in developing and understanding tools from applied mathematics and machine learning.

He obtained his master's degree in [Computer Science and Technology](https://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/csen/) in 2020 with [Prof. Yong Zhang](https://dagege.github.io/), his primary bachelor's degree in [Energy and Power Engineering](https://www.depe.tsinghua.edu.cn/depeen/) in 2017, and his secondary bachelor's degree in [Pure and Applied Mathematics](https://math.tsinghua.edu.cn) in 2018 with Prof. Hao Wu, all from [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/index.htm).
