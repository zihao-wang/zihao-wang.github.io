I am a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Yangqiu Song from CSE department, HKUST since Sept. 2020. The central theme of my current research is reasoning with symbolic knowledge. I am very interested in the highly related topics, including linguistics, learning, logic, computation and etc.

I obtained my master’s degree in Computer Science and Technology in 2020 with Prof. Yong Zhang, my primary bachelor’s degree in Energy and Power Engineering in 2017 with honor, and my secondary bachelor’s degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics in 2018 with Prof. Hao Wu, all from Tsinghua University.

I also work closely with my friends from HKUST, THU, and Tencent.
