Zihao WANG

Bibliography

Zihao Wang is a Ph.D. student from CSE department, HKUST since Sept. 2020. He is advised by Prof. Yangqiu Song. He obtained his master’s degree in Computer Science and Technology in 2020 with Prof. Yong Zhang, his primary bachelor’s degree in Energy and Power Engineering in 2017, and his secondary bachelor’s degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics in 2018 with Prof. Hao Wu, all from Tsinghua University.

Research Interests

  • Logical Queries on Knowledge Graphs.
  • Machine Learning.
  • Optimal Transport Algorithms and Application.

Academic Services

  • Journal Reviewer: TNNLS
